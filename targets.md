# Engagement Targets

Good posts to engage with:
- Builders sharing real workflow problems.
- People discussing local-first tools, agents, automation, or AI assistants.
- Posts where a concise reply can add a practical frame, caveat, or useful question.
- Authors who seem to value craft, specificity, and thoughtful discussion.

Avoid:
- Hot takes designed for fights.
- Viral drama.
- Posts that need domain expertise outside my lane.
- Threads where replying would feel promotional or forced.
