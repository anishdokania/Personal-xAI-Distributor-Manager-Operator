# Refund Policy

Personal X Operator is sold as a $20 one-time digital product.

## Suggested Policy

Refunds are available within 7 days if the buyer cannot install or run the tool after following the setup guide.

Refunds are not offered for:

- expected follower growth or engagement outcomes;
- X account limits, restrictions, or policy decisions;
- custom feature requests;
- buyer preference after successful installation;
- inability to use live automation safely on a specific X account.

## Support Boundary

The product is a local-first tool. Buyers are responsible for:

- their own machine setup;
- their X account;
- their API keys, if they choose OpenAI mode;
- actions posted through their account.
